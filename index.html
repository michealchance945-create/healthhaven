Welcome to the Health Haven Medical Centre

The Health Haven Medical Center is a dedicated non-profit organization focused on providing free, quality healthcare to the community. Our team of certified volunteer doctors delivers safe, reliable, and compassionate medical care to individuals and families who may not have access to private healthcare.

What Makes Our Healthcare Program Unique

Free Healthcare Services

We ensure that everyone — regardless of income — can receive proper medical attention.

Professional Volunteer Doctors

Our medical team consists of licensed professionals who generously volunteer their time and expertise.

24/7 Community Support

We remain available for urgent concerns and general health guidance around the clock.

Safe and Clean Environment

Our facility maintains a high standard of hygiene, safety, and medical professionalism.
