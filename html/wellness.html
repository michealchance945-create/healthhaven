Wellness Programs

Health Haven offers wellness programs to support physical, mental, and emotional health.

Our Wellness Services

  • Nutrition Guidance & Workshops:

    Practical healthy eating tips, personalized simple meal planning suggestions, and free nutrition education workshops open to all community members.

  • Accessible Fitness Support:

    Beginner-friendly exercise routines, stretching guidance, and adaptable fitness plans designed for all ages and abilities.

  • Mental Health & Stress Resilience Support:

    Guided relaxation activities, breathing exercises, and basic counseling focused on emotional well-being and stress management.

  • Public Health Awareness Sessions:

    Free monthly educational talks on disease prevention, hygiene, and healthy living techniques.
